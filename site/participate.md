# Participate

## On the boardwalk

The chalk station is on the Cygnet Boardwalk, at the midpoint of the marked 100-metre section. You'll find it — look for the timber box and the chalk dance steps on the bannisters.

**To participate:**

1. Pick up a piece of chalk from the station box
2. Follow the dance steps on the bannister — or invent your own
3. Respond to this week's prompt, or just add what's on your mind
4. Put the chalk back when you're done

There are no rules beyond that. You don't need to be an artist. You don't need to write anything legible. A line, a colour, a footprint — it all counts.

The surface resets with rain, and the dance steps are re-drawn within 24 hours of a washout. So if you come back tomorrow and your contribution is gone, that's not an ending — it's the palimpsest at work.

## Share a photo

Did you add something to the boardwalk today? We'd love to include it in the community gallery.

Use your phone's camera to scan the QR code at the chalk station, or [submit your photo here](#photo-form).

**What we do with your photo:**

- It will be reviewed before publication
- It may appear in the online gallery at this site

## The Happiness Index

Near the chalk station you'll find a simple chalk bar graph on the walkway surface, marked 0 to 10. Each day, draw a bar showing how happy you're feeling right now. It takes about five seconds.

The graph is wiped clean each morning and re-drawn fresh. Your bar is one data point in a running record of how walkers on this boardwalk are feeling across the eight weeks of the project.

You don't have to explain it. You don't have to sign it. Just draw the bar.
