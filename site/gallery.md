# Gallery

## The chalk archive

This gallery documents the evolving surface of the Cygnet Boardwalk over the eight weeks of the Palimpsest Path project. Photographs are taken each morning as part of the daily site audit and supplemented by photos submitted by participants.

The archive is organised by week. Each photograph is a complete daily snapshot — a single moment in the palimpsest cycle.

---

*The project has not yet started — it is currently awaiting Huon Valley Council permit approval and University of Tasmania academic oversight confirmation. The gallery will be populated once the project begins.*

*Check back here for updates, or [get in touch](/contact) if you'd like to be notified at launch.*

## Submit your photo

Add your own contribution to the archive. Scan the QR code at the chalk station or visit the [Participate](/participate) page.

---

## Community stories

The gallery is more than photographs — it is also a record of what the boardwalk means to the people who walk it. If you have a photo and a few words to go with it, you can share your story here.

Visit the [Participate](/participate) page to submit a photo and caption. Community stories are reviewed before publication and nothing is posted without your consent.

You can also [write a longer post](/ideas) — a memory, an observation, a response to one of the chalk prompts. Posts submitted through the site are reviewed and published in the Community Stories section of the blog.
